Moonlight Greasword

Information

The legendary large sword that reflects moonlight.
Widely known in association with Moonlight Knight Vito.
It's one of the few Revelations from God.
The blue crystal blade is composed only of light,
so it's impossible to block with a shield.

Characteristics:

  • Greasword
  • 228 AC
